Diving into the vibrant world of the Ringling Circus was a thrilling adventure! From Florida to Louisiana and all the way to Cleveland, I got to capture the magic of their upcoming two-year tour. It was a massive project, just like the show itself. As I snapped away to fulfill a million and three photo requests, I couldn't resist the urge to create GIFs. They became the perfect way to bring to life those electrifying moments that were too dynamic for still images.
