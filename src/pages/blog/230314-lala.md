Flying into LAX is usually ordinary and depressing. But this time was different. Amidst the urban chaos, there were unexpected touches of beauty. Spring had sprung, and patches of wildflowers burst forth from the cracks in the runway, their vibrant colors starkly contrasting the drab surroundings. Snow-capped mountains rose in the distance, a reminder of the untamed wilderness beyond the melancholic beauty of the City of Angels.
