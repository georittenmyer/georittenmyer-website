Another August, another Disney on Ice production. This year's show — "Jump In" — brought a new setlist, relentless energy, and some of the most visually dynamic skating I've had the pleasure of shooting. Two weeks on the ice in Orlando, followed by a month of retouching, all made possible by the incredible production team at Disney on Ice. It's one of those gigs that's equal parts demanding and deeply satisfying. Already looking forward to next summer.
