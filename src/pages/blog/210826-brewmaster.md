The art of making beer has become one the most popular mediums in the last ten years. In fact, the neighborhood I live in has 13 breweries and counting. I collaborated with Bad Jimmy's Brewing to showcase the brewing process. I was able to test out some new technology by creating motion using high-speed strobe lights and still photography. Overall the brewing process is similar in ways to the commercial photographer's craft. Lots of preparation and hurrying up to wait, resulting in something that sells and engages the audience.
