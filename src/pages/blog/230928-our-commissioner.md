SeattleMet asked me to get a photo of my Public Lands Commissioner for the spring issue in December. Hilary Franz is magical, eager to hike a mile wearing high heels and a peacoat to the selected overlook despite the cold. She stopped to admire and climb a towering tree and shared stories of raising her boys on a farm and figuring skating as a child. She has my support for Governor.
