Projects like The Fictitious Life of Kat Bell allow me to play with conceptual storytelling. I enjoy mixing high levels of post production with studio work and landscapes to create scenes that can't always be captured on location. This has been helpful for clients during COVID as big production sets aren't necessary to execute these types of shoots. I have been working on some personal projects geared toward self promotion. With my turnkey production solutions, I am able to produce high quality imaginative content on a low budget. This series profiles Kat, a fictitious character who is trying to balance life in a collegiate world while keeping her magical past a secret. This was an opportunity to get creative while working independently to create a cinematic story with a wonderful person.
