A few years ago, some incredible people started a semi-pro, hyper-local soccer club right here in the neighborhood, and it's been amazing to watch it grow. Now they've done it again, launching a sister club — a semi-pro women's team called Salmon Bay FC.
I got a last-minute request to help with their media day, and I was happy to contribute some personal creative shots to the mix.
I'm genuinely grateful for the community and the people working to make this neighborhood better. This one felt good to be a part of.
