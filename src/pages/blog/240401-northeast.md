This year's midwinter break was a memorable adventure as our family headed back East to reunite with our recently removed Seattle friends. We spent a week embracing the beauty of Catskill living, enjoying cozy days, and East Coast snowboarding on ribbons of manufactured powder. The trip ended with an exciting weekend in the city, where we explored iconic sights and soaked in the lively atmosphere. Every moment, from the tick-ridden mountains to the smelly streets, reminded us of how special these trips are with this close-knit group of friends.
