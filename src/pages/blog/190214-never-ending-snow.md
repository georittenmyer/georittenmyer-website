The snow in Seattle has been reprehensible over the past month. I love snow, but this is unlike any winter I've experienced in this city. The streets are frozen and school has been canceled for weeks. Seattle's winter is not supposed to be like this and the city lacks the operations to manage such extremes. Maybe it was cabin fever, maybe it was creative angst, but I took an evening stroll with a camera. Not sure what it accomplished, but here is a glimpse of a city blanketed and cold as I wandered through my hood.
