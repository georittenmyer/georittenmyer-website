For several days, I set my alarm for 3am to wake up and check for clear night skies through the window..and willingly go back to sleep when I saw clouds. Then came a clear day and a clear night, I couldn't fall asleep so I just turned my alarm off at 2:55 am and headed out to try and find the new comet Neowise. I had already researched and planned the general location of where I wanted to try and capture a photo. I just needed to actually find the comet in the sky and line up the foreground. I had been prepping for a week and waiting a lifetime for the chance to photograph the Space Needle with a comet. And thus the night sky and the city lights provided. Mission Complete.
