I've spent a lot more time exploring local parks and properties lately. Maybe I'm taking more time to smell the local roses, or maybe there's a worldwide pandemic keeping me from flying all over the world this summer. I found a view above a tennis court on one of these explorations. I liked the photo, shared it with my wifey and she suggested I push it further by adding a character portrait. We reached out to a friend in our covid bubble to pose as a god fearing, bad ass tennis star. So in honor of the US Open Tennis tournament taking place in a bubble this year, I took a photo of a friend in my bubble. Love-all
