I once again attended the Palm Springs Photo Festival, my 3rd year in a row. I had been planning on attending but not necessarily taking a workshop, that was until a friend texted me saying that Nadav Kander was leading one. I signed up immediately. The workshop was transformative, Nadav was brilliant and the students inspiring. I am not sure what else to say about the experience besides it changed my life. I do believe my personal vision will change and not because I'm looking for the next, but rather I have looked at my past and what got me to this point. Thank you, Nadav. These are just some random photos from the week, the workshop was non-shooting.
