Another seasonal shoot with Disney on Ice! I've worked with this production for nearly 20 years, and it never gets old. This year brought a unique challenge: the show featured a variety of Disney properties and a vibrant EDM-inspired theme. It pushed me to infuse more movement and energy into the still images, resulting in one of my favorite Disney shoots to date.
