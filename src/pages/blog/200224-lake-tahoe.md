My favorite way to celebrate President's Day is in South Lake Tahoe. I've spent numerous 3-day weekends in February in the small mountain town. After a three year hiatus, the opportunity to return came again. I took the first couple days to ride Heavenly Resort and on the third day I took photos in familiar places with some of my favorite faces.
