Amazon commissioned me to shoot ultra-wide architectural photography for their summit meeting screens... all in one afternoon. I prepped like crazy (building layouts, sun calculators, meeting room availability calendars), partly because I had to, mostly because I love that kind of puzzle. The weather wasn't perfect, but everything else lined up. Shoot went great, and now those images are on screens at their big events.
