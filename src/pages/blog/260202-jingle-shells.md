A last-minute job pulled me to Florida for the holidays — a shoot on the 26th and another on the 31st, which meant bringing the whole family along. While I was working, they did their own exploring. But the time I had between shoots? Pure Florida. Sun, beach, and that particular kind of warm-weather December that makes you forget the rest of the world exists. It had been a while since I'd spent the holiday season in the Sunshine State, and doing it with family made it something worth remembering.
