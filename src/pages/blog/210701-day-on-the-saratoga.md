I headed out on a whale watching adventure on a fairly typical early summer northwest manic weather day. Wind, rain, some lighting, and of course, sunshine were all on tap. Orcas, minke whales, and puffins were all enjoying the weather as well. The Saratoga is a custom whale watching boat and maybe one of the more amazing boats I've been on, and I highly recommend it.
