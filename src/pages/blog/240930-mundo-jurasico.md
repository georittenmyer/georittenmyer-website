Over the summer, I had the privilege of refreshing the photography assets for the Jurassic World Exhibition. The project brought me to vibrant Mexico City, where I spent several days collaborating with the marketing team at NeonGlobal to capture a lovely catalog of imagery. Working in CDMX is always a rewarding experience—its talent, expertise, and resources are truly world-class.
