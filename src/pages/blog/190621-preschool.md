One of my favorite things about parenting in Seattle is that I can enroll my child in an outdoor preschool. Come rain or shine, their classroom is entirely outside and they learn by exploring the nature around them. My son's classroom just happens to also be the Seattle Art Museums, Olympic Sculpture Park making his daily surroundings fueled by creativity and nature. Inspired by the astounding artist Richard Serra's sculpture, I set up among "Wake" and utilized its structure as the background for a set of unique class photos.
