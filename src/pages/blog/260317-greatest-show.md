Something I thought would never happen again did. For years, my holiday season meant sunny Florida and the circus, those winter quarters when the show would go offline, bring in new acts, and rehearse the magic behind the scenes. Then Ringling decided to rework the show over the holidays and premiere the first weekend of the year, and suddenly I was back. Two weeks of shooting, collaborating with every department imaginable, building out a massive collection of imagery for their upcoming tour. It felt like coming home to something I never expected to see again.
