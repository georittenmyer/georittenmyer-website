I love quick portrait shoots, especially when the person actually wants to be photographed and comes in with their own ideas. It's even better when they're genuinely interesting and kind. And to top it off, when they know your hometown and people you grew up with, it makes the whole experience that much more fun. This shoot was for The London Times, accompanying a whistleblower piece on Meta. There's a lot more behind these photos than just a beautiful face...
