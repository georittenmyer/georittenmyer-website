Geo Rittenmyer

Driven by the dance of light and the allure of narrative, Geo Rittenmyer is a visual artist trained at the Brooks Institute of Photography.

His commercial work spans editorial, advertising, and live event photography — with clients including ESPN, Sports Illustrated, Ralph Lauren, Disney, NBC Universal, and major advertising agencies.

Current projects include a personal landscape book combining photography with observations on global affairs. Based in Seattle with a fully equipped photo studio.

Stock imagery available through Gallery Stock.
